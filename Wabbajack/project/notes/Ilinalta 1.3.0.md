## Ilinalta Modlist Review (v1.3.x - Current)

**Ilinalta** is a lightweight, **pure vanilla visual enhancement** Wabbajack modlist (~100–200 mods) designed for players who want better graphics without changing gameplay, adding SKSE plugins, or breaking vanilla saves. Created for maximum compatibility (including Skyrim Together multiplayer), it uses high-quality texture/mesh replacers, ENB, and ReShade while requiring **zero ESPs or SKSE mods**.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Pure Vanilla Visual Remaster**.  
- No gameplay frameworks (not Requiem, EnaiRim, SimonRim, or anything else).  
- Philosophy: Enhance Skyrim's looks while keeping the game 100% vanilla in mechanics, progression, combat, quests, and balance. Perfect for players who love the original experience but want it to look significantly better. Fully compatible with unmodded saves and Skyrim Together multiplayer.

### 2. Combat System
**Completely vanilla combat**.  
- No animation overhauls, dodging frameworks, MCO, BFCO, Wildcat, Blade & Blunt, or any combat mods.  
- Only minor bug fixes if needed. Combat remains exactly as Bethesda intended — familiar and unchanged. Ideal for classic playstyles or adding your own combat mods later.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**Strong focus on clean, enhanced visuals**.  
- High-quality texture and mesh replacers for landscapes, flora, architecture, NPCs, and weapons.  
- Carefully tuned **ENB + ReShade** for improved lighting, colors, and atmosphere.  
- **Performance**: Extremely lightweight and accessible.  
  - Runs well even on mid-to-low hardware (GTX 1060 / 16 GB RAM at 1080p/1440p with good FPS).  
  - Very small install size. One of the most performant visual lists available.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Strictly vanilla**.  
- No new quests, lands, Legacy of the Dragonborn, or content additions.  
- Vanilla story, factions, and exploration are untouched — enhanced purely through better visuals and immersion.

### 5. Cities & World
**Visually upgraded vanilla world**.  
- Textures, lighting, and details improved across cities, landscapes, interiors, and environments without altering layouts or adding population mods.  
- The world feels fresher and more detailed while preserving the original structure, density, and atmosphere perfectly.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Outstanding stability** — one of the cleanest lists possible.  
- Zero ESP/SKSE conflicts; extremely reliable and easy to maintain. Perfect for multiplayer with Skyrim Together.  
- **Difficulty**: Pure vanilla — completely unchanged and approachable for everyone.

### Verdict
**Ilinalta is best suited for players who want a beautiful visual upgrade to vanilla Skyrim that remains fully compatible with unmodded saves and Skyrim Together, without any gameplay changes.**  
It’s an excellent lightweight choice for mid-to-low end hardware, classic Skyrim fans, multiplayer groups, or anyone seeking a "Skyrim but prettier" experience. More minimal and compatible than heavier visual foundations like NGVO, Anvil, or Krentoraan, and perfect as a base for adding your own mods later. A great option in 2026 for purists who prioritize stability, multiplayer compatibility, and clean visuals over combat or content overhauls.
