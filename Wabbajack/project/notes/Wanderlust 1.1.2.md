## Wanderlust Modlist Review (v1.0.x - Current)

**Wanderlust** (by NyghtTones) is a **hardcore Requiem survival/roleplaying** Wabbajack modlist that serves as the spiritual successor to the author's previous list *Krahven*. It delivers a gritty, immersive, low-to-mid fantasy experience with streamlined content, improved visuals, and punishing gameplay. Released in 1.0 around early 2026, it focuses on stability, meaningful progression, and a dangerous Tamriel where exploration and preparation are key.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Requiem-based hardcore survival RPG** with custom tweaks.  
- Built around **Requiem** as the foundation (deleveling, meaningful progression, no hand-holding).  
- Includes **3BFTweaks** (and similar modern adjustments) for better balance and quality-of-life without softening the core challenge.  
- Philosophy: A refined, streamlined hardcore experience. You start weak and must earn everything through smart play, crafting, and roleplaying. Focus on immersion, exploration, and a living world rather than massive bloat or new lands (priority on vanilla stability first).

### 2. Combat System
**Tactical, dangerous, and realistic**.  
- Core: **Requiem** combat with enhanced AI, hit reactions, and enemy behaviors.  
- Supporting mods for better animations, timing, and responsiveness.  
- Combat is punishing, stamina-focused, and high-stakes — fights require scouting, preparation, and skill. No flashy MCO power fantasy; it feels grounded and brutal, rewarding careful playstyles (melee, archery, magic).

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**Atmospheric and improved visuals** (upgraded from previous lists).  
- Tuned ENB + ReShade with graphical fidelity and performance profiles. Enhanced textures, lighting/weather, and landscapes that support the gritty survival tone.  
- **Performance**: Optimized with multiple profiles.  
  - Runs on mid-to-high hardware (RTX 3060+ recommended for smooth play). Better optimized than many large Requiem lists thanks to streamlining.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Streamlined vanilla+ with curated additions**.  
- Vanilla quests are overhauled for better pacing and integration.  
- Handpicked new encounters, followers, and custom content that fit Requiem without overwhelming the base game. No massive new worldspaces in initial versions — focus is on making vanilla feel fresh and dangerous. Strong emphasis on exploration and discovery.

### 5. Cities & World
**Dangerous, immersive, and cohesive world**.  
- The world feels alive and threatening through improved encounters, dynamic elements, and survival mechanics.  
- Settlements and landscapes are enhanced for atmosphere while respecting vanilla structure. Exploration is central, risky, and highly rewarding.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Strong stability focus** for a Requiem list, with ongoing updates.  
- Streamlined design helps reduce conflicts; active author support.  
- **Difficulty**: **Very hardcore** — early game is brutal (permadeath optional in some playstyles). Demands patience, planning, and roleplaying. Extremely rewarding for dedicated survival/RP fans.

### Verdict
**Wanderlust is best suited for experienced Requiem players who want a refined, streamlined hardcore survival experience with strong immersion and a dangerous Tamriel on mid-to-high end hardware.**  
It improves on the author's previous work with better visuals, stability, and balance while staying true to punishing old-school RPG roots. More accessible/streamlined than some ultra-bloated Requiem lists, more survival-focused than power fantasy options, and a great choice for players who enjoy slow-burn progression, exploration, and realistic hardship. A solid newcomer in 2026 for fans of gritty, lore-friendly challenges — check the author's trailers and Discord for the latest details!
