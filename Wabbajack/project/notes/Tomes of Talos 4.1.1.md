## Tomes of Talos (TOT) Modlist Review (v4.x - Current)

**Tomes of Talos** (by Herr Schtevie / The Modding Bordello) is a **lore-rich, SFW power fantasy** Wabbajack modlist (~3200+ mods). It is a clean, immersive fork of *Journals of Jyggalag* (JOJ), designed for players who want stunning visuals, expanded content, and refined gameplay without any adult/NSFW elements. It delivers a darker, deeper, and more dangerous Tamriel while remaining highly stable and performant.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Power fantasy with strong EnaiRim foundation** (not Requiem or pure SimonRim).  
- Heavy use of the **full EnaiRim suite** (Ordinator, Apocalypse, Odin, Andromeda, etc.) for flavorful, powerful progression and build variety.  
- Philosophy: Create a cohesive, lore-friendly adventure with high replayability. Focus on storytelling, exploration, and immersion in a visually upgraded, content-expanded Skyrim. Built for intensity and depth without extreme hardcore simulation.

### 2. Combat System
**Modern cinematic action combat** with excellent flexibility.  
- Core: **BFCO** (Attack Behavior Framework) with **For Honor / Elden Rim** movesets (and optional Wolong movesets via in-game ring).  
- Features True Directional Movement, stamina-based dodging/rolling, Pandora animations, and tuned enemy AI.  
- Combat is challenging yet fair and highly satisfying across all playstyles (mage, rogue, battlemage, berserker, dual-wield). Fluid, weighty, and responsive — one of the strongest aspects of the list.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**Gorgeous and well-optimized visuals** — a major highlight.  
- **ENB**: Cabbage ENB (Lord’s Vision profile) with multiple presets. Full Lux lighting, Obsidian Weathers, high-res textures, complex parallax, lush flora, and detailed landscapes.  
- Two profiles: **Lord’s Vision** (full graphics/ENB) and **Performance** (Community Shaders for better FPS on mid-range systems).  
- **Performance**: Surprisingly good for the mod count. Runs well on mid-to-high hardware (RTX 3060/4070+ recommended for smooth 1440p). Install size substantial but optimized with strong stability.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Massive curated content expansion** — one of its biggest strengths.  
- Includes **Legacy of the Dragonborn**, VIGILANT, Project AHO, Wyrmstooth, The Forgotten City, Gray Cowl of Nocturnal, Sirenroot, Unslaad, Carved Brink, and many more high-quality quests/new lands.  
- Vanilla quests are enhanced and seamlessly integrated. Tons of new gear, spells, followers, and lore-rich adventures. Everything feels cohesive and story-driven.

### 5. Cities & World
**Significantly enhanced and detailed world**.  
- Uses **JK’s Skyrim**, Cities of the North, The Great Cities, and other high-quality overhauls for more populated, immersive settlements.  
- Lush, region-specific landscapes with dense forests, improved lighting, and atmospheric depth. The world feels alive, dangerous, and rewarding to explore.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Excellent stability** — frequently praised as clean, well-patched, and reliable.  
- Active Discord support and regular updates. Minimal issues reported on current versions.  
- **Difficulty**: Power fantasy leaning but challenging and engaging. Combat demands skill and preparation; builds feel strong and rewarding as you progress. Adjustable and viable for many playstyles.

### Verdict
**Tomes of Talos is best suited for players with mid-to-high end hardware who want a beautiful, content-packed, lore-friendly power fantasy Skyrim experience without any NSFW elements.**  
It excels as a stable, immersive "next-gen" adventure with fantastic EnaiRim progression, modern combat, stunning visuals, and tons of well-integrated quests. More content-heavy and visually polished than many Vanilla+ lists, less punishing than Requiem-based ones like LoreRim, and a great SFW alternative to its sibling Journals of Jyggalag. Perfect for story lovers, explorers, and action fans seeking a cohesive, replayable Tamriel in 2026. Highly recommended as one of the strong mid-2025/2026 releases!
