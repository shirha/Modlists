## Vagabond Remastered Modlist Review (v0.5.x+ - Current, ~3000–4500+ mods)

**Vagabond Remastered** (by ylikollikas / Oghma-Infinium, with Biggie Boss involvement) is a massive, next-gen Wabbajack modlist that returns the popular original Vagabond with updated visuals, modern combat, and heavy content. It focuses on a **challenging action RPG experience** with stunning graphics, Legacy of the Dragonborn integration, and a dark-yet-epic tone. It's known for pushing hardware limits while delivering one of the most visually impressive Skyrim experiences available.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Modern action RPG with EnaiRim power fantasy**.  
- Heavy **EnaiRim suite** + **Vokriinator Black** (Ordinator + Vokrii hybrid) for flavorful, powerful, and versatile builds.  
- Philosophy: A "next-gen Skyrim" with modern combat, diverse world, survival elements, and high replayability. Strong focus on visuals, exploration, and feeling like a skilled vagabond-turned-legend in a dangerous Tamriel. Includes full seasons support and extensive monster/gear variety.

### 2. Combat System
**Modern Souls-like / Elden Ring-inspired action combat** — a core strength.  
- Heavy use of **MCO/ADXP** with advanced animations, dodging, timing, poise, and weapon-specific movesets.  
- Combat is committed, weighty, and dynamic — third-person focused with aggressive enemies and satisfying feedback.  
- Supports melee, magic, and hybrid playstyles well. More action-oriented and flashy than pure tactical lists while remaining challenging.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**One of the most graphically intensive lists** — next-gen visuals are its hallmark.  
- High-end ENB (often Cabbage or custom), full parallax/PBR textures, lush/detailed landscapes, advanced lighting/weather, seasons, and premium NPC/creature overhauls.  
- **Performance**: Extremely demanding (often called "PC-melting").  
  - Minimum: RTX 4080/4090+ / 32+ GB RAM / high VRAM (12GB+ recommended).  
  - Install size: 400–850+ GB total space needed. Best at 1440p with DLSS/Frame Generation. Not for mid-range hardware.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Significant expansion with Legacy of the Dragonborn focus**.  
- Strong **LotD** integration alongside many new quests, lands, monsters, weapons, armors, and locations.  
- Vanilla quests are enhanced for better flow. The world feels diverse and alive with new encounters and variety.

### 5. Cities & World
**Enhanced and visually spectacular world**.  
- Major visual overhauls to cities, settlements, and landscapes for a dense, immersive feel.  
- Full seasons change the environment dramatically. Exploration is rewarding with new content and breathtaking scenery.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Generally stable for its massive size** with ongoing Remastered updates, though large lists can have occasional issues.  
- Active community support via Discord/GitHub.  
- **Difficulty**: Challenging early-to-mid game (Souls-like feel) that transitions into power fantasy. Demands skill, preparation, and good builds. Adjustable but geared toward dedicated players.

### Verdict
**Vagabond Remastered is best suited for players with high-end hardware (RTX 4080/4090 class or better) who want a visually breathtaking, modern action RPG Skyrim with EnaiRim progression, Souls-inspired combat, and massive content.**  
It excels as a "next-gen" showcase with epic scope and beauty, more visually and combat-focused than many lists. Similar in ambition to Nolvus or Wunduniik but with stronger EnaiRim/Vokriinator flavor and LotD emphasis. Demanding on your PC and patience, but delivers an unforgettable experience for those who can run it. A standout visual and action powerhouse in 2026 — perfect for screenshot enthusiasts and skilled action fans!
