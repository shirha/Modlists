## ElderTeej Modlist Review (v4.0.x - Current, ~2000+ mods)

**ElderTeej** (by tpartridge89 / Teej) is a popular Wabbajack modlist that evolved from a fork into its own polished identity. It is a **LOTD-centric power fantasy** list with strong **Community Shaders + PBR visuals**, modern BFCO combat, and heavy emphasis on collection, museum filling, and fun gameplay. v4.0 was a major rebuild focusing on stability, reduced bloat, and better performance while keeping the core "fill the museum" vibe.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**LOTD Power Fantasy with mixed modern overhauls**.  
- Heavy focus on **Legacy of the Dragonborn** (every supported display, follower, and quest integrated).  
- Uses a mix of **Mysticism + Vokrii/Vokord** (perk overhauls), vanilla skill leveling with **Experience** mod, and custom tweaks for progression.  
- Philosophy: A fun, collectathon-heavy experience where you feel like a powerful Dragonborn filling a massive museum. Includes QoL, safer saving, and curated content without extreme hardcore simulation or Requiem deleveling.

### 2. Combat System
**Modern BFCO action combat** — tweaked and highly enjoyable.  
- Core: **BFCO** (Attack Behavior Framework) with custom animations, stances, dodging, and improved feel.  
- Enhanced with Rogues n Raiders and The Restless Dead for more visceral enemy behavior.  
- Combat is responsive, satisfying, and power-fantasy friendly — timing blocks and positioning matter, but you grow strong and flashy. Great for all playstyles with good magic/archery support.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**Strong modern visuals powered by Community Shaders**.  
- Full PBR texture suite, complex parallax, high-quality landscapes/flora, enhanced lighting/weather, and detailed NPC overhauls. Often paired with Cabbage ENB options.  
- **Performance**: Significantly improved in v4.0 (70-80 GB smaller than previous versions).  
  - Runs well on mid-to-high hardware (RTX 3060/4070+ recommended for smooth 1440p). Multiple performance tweaks available. Install size much more reasonable now.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Heavy LOTD and collection focus** — a major strength.  
- Maximizes **Legacy of the Dragonborn** integration with tons of supported quests, items, and followers.  
- Curated vanilla enhancements plus additional high-quality quests and content that feed into the museum/collection loop.  
- Exploration and relic hunting are extremely rewarding with plenty to discover and display.

### 5. Cities & World
**Enhanced and detailed world** with visual and population improvements.  
- Cities and settlements feel more alive and detailed through textures, lighting, and additions.  
- Landscapes are lush and immersive with PBR upgrades. The world supports the collector/adventurer fantasy with seamless integration of new content.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Greatly improved stability** in v4.0 rebuild.  
- Bloat removed, thorough patching, and active maintenance. Users report it as one of the more stable large lists. Active Discord support.  
- **Difficulty**: Power fantasy — challenging early but becomes rewarding and fun as you progress and collect. Not hardcore Requiem level; more approachable with adjustable elements.

### Verdict
**ElderTeej is best suited for players who love Legacy of the Dragonborn, museum collecting, modern BFCO combat, and solid PBR visuals on mid-to-high end hardware.**  
It delivers a fun, content-rich power fantasy experience centered around filling the museum while feeling modern and polished. More collectathon-focused and accessible than heavy visual beasts like Nolvus or Wunduniik, less punishing than LoreRim, and a great choice for players who enjoy progression through exploration and relics. v4.0's rebuild made it leaner and more enjoyable — a strong recommendation in 2026 for LOTD fans and action-oriented collectors!
