## Viir Su'um (ViirSuum) Modlist Review (Current - ~1700+ mods)

**Viir Su'um** (often stylized ViirSuum) is a **lightweight modern Requiem** Wabbajack modlist by a dedicated author, frequently described as a "slimmed-down LoreRim" with strong NGVO visuals. It combines hardcore Requiem progression with modern combat polish, excellent performance, and a clean, cohesive experience. It appeals to players wanting challenging survival/RPG depth without the massive bloat of heavier lists.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Modern lightweight Requiem**.  
- Built firmly on **Requiem** as the core (deleveling, meaningful progression, preparation required).  
- Philosophy: A snappy, modern-feeling Requiem experience with high visual quality and streamlined mods. It takes the spirit of LoreRim (lore-friendly + modern action) but stays lighter and more performant. Strong emphasis on exploration, crafting, and roleplaying in a dangerous world.

### 2. Combat System
**Modern action with Requiem tactics**.  
- Uses **MCO/ADXP** elements or similar modern animation frameworks for fluid, responsive third-person combat.  
- Combined with Requiem's punishing balance, Precision, improved AI, and timing mechanics.  
- Combat feels snappy, weighty, and satisfying — more modern and engaging than classic Requiem while retaining tactical depth. Good support for melee, magic, and archery builds.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**NGVO-based stunning visuals** (thanks to Biggie Boss influence).  
- Cabbage ENB or similar high-quality presets, full parallax, enhanced textures, lush landscapes, and atmospheric lighting/weather.  
- **Performance**: One of the strongest points — runs surprisingly well for a Requiem list.  
  - Excellent on mid-range hardware (60 FPS at 1440p/2K easily reported).  
  - Much lighter than 3000+ mod behemoths. Great optimization makes it accessible.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Curated vanilla+ with new quests**.  
- Vanilla quests enhanced for better integration and pacing.  
- Includes select high-quality new quests, creatures, gear, and encounters that feel lore-friendly.  
- Focus on exploration and crafting rather than overwhelming content bloat — the world feels fresh and rewarding without excessive additions.

### 5. Cities & World
**Enhanced immersive world** with NGVO visuals.  
- Landscapes and settlements benefit from premium textures, lighting, and details.  
- The world feels alive, dangerous, and cohesive while respecting vanilla structure. Exploration is highly encouraged and punishingly fun.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Good stability** for a Requiem list, with positive community feedback on smoothness.  
- Active development and patching. Some users note it's still maturing but very playable.  
- **Difficulty**: Hardcore Requiem-style — early game is brutal and demands preparation. Rewarding for dedicated players who enjoy challenge and growth.

### Verdict
**Viir Su'um is best suited for players who want a modern, performant Requiem experience with beautiful NGVO visuals, snappy combat, and a lighter footprint on mid-range hardware.**  
It stands out as a "snappiest" Requiem list — like a streamlined LoreRim with excellent visuals and better accessibility. Ideal for those who love hardcore survival/RPG depth but dislike massive installs or poor performance. Less visually extreme than Nolvus/Wunduniik, more modern than older Requiem lists like Wildlander, and a strong recommendation in 2026 for fans of challenging yet polished Tamriel adventures.
