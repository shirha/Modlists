## Apostasy Modlist Review (v3.x - Current, ~3000+ mods)

**Apostasy** (by Aljo / Oghma-Infinium) is a premium Wabbajack modlist that delivers a **modern, cohesive Skyrim experience** with stunning high-fidelity visuals and action-oriented gameplay. Developed with input from creators behind Arisen, Ascensio, Fahluaan, and Vagabond, it emphasizes refinement, immersion, and a power fantasy feel while staying lore-friendly. Frequently praised for its beauty, smooth performance relative to its size, and unique custom mods.

### 1. Core Gameplay Philosophy
**Modern action RPG overhaul on a Vanilla+ base** (custom framework, not pure Requiem, EnaiRim, or SimonRim).  
- Heavy focus on custom-tailored gameplay systems, including a unique **Paragon perk overhaul** for combat/stealth and custom perks for magic.  
- Philosophy: Modernize Skyrim into a next-gen RPG without breaking its soul — responsive combat, expanded content, and high visual fidelity. Strong power fantasy progression with custom mechanics built specifically for the list.

### 2. Combat System
**Modern cinematic action combat** — a major highlight with custom elements.  
- Core: **MCO/ADXP** + **Stances NG**, TK Dodge, Maxsu Poise, and custom animations/movesets (four different stances per weapon type).  
- Supports seamless first- and third-person play with fluid dodging, timing, combos, and visceral feedback. Highly responsive, satisfying, and versatile for melee, magic, and hybrid builds. More action-oriented and fun than pure tactical lists, with excellent polish.

### 3. Visuals & Performance
**One of the best-looking lists available** — consistently called breathtaking.  
- High-fidelity setup with **NAT III** weather, full **Lux / GGUnit's Lux Suite**, complex parallax, premium textures, lush/gritty landscapes, detailed cities/interiors, and cohesive NPC overhauls. Strong low-fantasy/dark Nordic atmosphere.  
- **Performance**: Surprisingly well-optimized for its scale (runs smoother than many comparable heavy lists).  
  - Recommended: RTX 4070+ / 32 GB RAM for excellent 1440p/4K results. Install size substantial but optimized with good frame rates on high-end hardware.

### 4. Quests & Content
**Significant curated expansions**.  
- Enhanced vanilla quests plus new quests, lands, weapons, armors, followers, and locations that integrate naturally.  
- Expanded cities/towns and new adventures add depth and replayability. Everything feels lore-friendly and purposeful rather than random bloat.

### 5. Cities & World
**Expanded and enhanced world** with premium details.  
- Uses high-quality overhauls (Great Towns and Cities, RedBag's Skyrim, custom interiors) for denser, more lived-in settlements.  
- Landscapes and environments feel dynamic, atmospheric, and visually stunning while respecting vanilla structure. Exploration is highly rewarding.

### 6. Stability & Difficulty
**Excellent stability** for its size, thanks to thorough custom patching.  
- Well-maintained with active support; minimal issues reported on current versions.  
- **Difficulty**: Power fantasy leaning — challenging but fair and fun, with strong progression. Not as punishing as Requiem lists; more accessible and rewarding for most players.

### Verdict
**Apostasy is best suited for players with high-end hardware who want a visually spectacular, modern action RPG Skyrim with polished custom combat, strong immersion, and cohesive enhancements.**  
It stands out as one of the top "next-gen" lists in 2026 — often called more beautiful than many competitors while delivering fun, responsive gameplay and meaningful content. More action-oriented and visually refined than Nordic Souls or Gate to Sovngarde, less hardcore than LoreRim, and a step above many kitchen-sink lists in polish and vision. Perfect for players seeking a fresh, addictive, and gorgeous Tamriel experience. Highly recommended as a standout choice!
